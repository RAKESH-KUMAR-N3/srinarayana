Teaching Methodology

At Sri Narayana, we believe in a conceptual approach to learning. Our methodology includes:

  • Concept-Based Learning: Moving beyond rote memorization to deep understanding of subjects.
  • Regular Practice Tests: Weekly assessments to track progress and improve time management.
  • Performance Tracking: Detailed analytics for each student to identify strengths and weaknesses.
  • Interactive Sessions: Open forums for doubt clearance and peer learning.

Sri Narayana is a reputed medical and engineering coaching institute in Hyderabad, and we offer coaching for all entrance examinations.
